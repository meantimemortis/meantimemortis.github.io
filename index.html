Welcome to Echoes of Sanson

Even in school, I was fascinated by France’s dark and violent past — a history bound with blood and public executions. Searching for more, I discovered how rare the name Sanson truly is, and how difficult it is to uncover personal traces of this family. I created this site to gather those echoes — fragments of memory, pieces of stories, and the traces left behind.

The Executioner’s Legacy

Charles-Henri Sanson, the most infamous of the dynasty, presided over the guillotine during the French Revolution. His story is one of contradiction: duty and revulsion, survival and stigma. Here, I will collect fragments of his life and those who bore his name.
